Diversity and Inclusion in the workplace

Everyone in the workplace has a right to experiencing diversity and inclusion. Read on below to find out more, and how you can help both your employees and your business!

Information

Diversity

Diversity is about the range of employees in your company. This can vary from people of different ages, religions, ethnicities, genders and people with disabilities. Diversity also includes valuing these differences.

Inclusion

Inclusion is about letting everyone feel that their voices are heard at work. It lets all employees feel like they're able to contribute with different ideas, raise issues or suggestions they might have, and even attempt to optimise workflow within the company.

Equality

Both diversity and inclusion lead to a feeling of equality in the workplace. This fosters less resentment between employees and an overall happier and more producitve workforce.

Concerns

Many are concerned that the work to improve diversity and inclusion is merely for show. By educating yourself and taking steps to implement what you learn, you can allay these fears and foster greater trust within your company.

Best practices for fostering and maintaining diversity and inclusion

  • Set measurable diversity goals
  • Create ways to allow employees to open up about what's wrong
  • Encourage people to get talking
  • Develop written policies
  • Maintain regular staff training
